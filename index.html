Welcome to Banjara Tola Camp

 Reinvigorate, Re-energize , Relax
Banjara Tola Camp is a forest camp next to ‘River Heval’, built in the serene landscape and magnetic charm of holy city Rishikesh. At Banjara Tola Camp an extraordinary holiday awaits you under starry nights and sunlit mornings at this camp surrounded by stunning and beautiful hills. A combined excursion of forest camping along with the adourned charm of Heval River is waiting for you to enjoy to the fullest. Camping amidst forest & indulging in variety of activities that can be conducted inside and outside the campus. So get ready to be astonished by the scenic appeal and peaceful atmosphere of this entire place where camping takes place with great fun and adventure.
